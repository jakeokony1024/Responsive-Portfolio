About Me

Hello! my name is Jacob Okony and I was born in Aurora Colorado, but I grew up in Michigan as my parents moved back to where they were from after three years. I spent the next 19 years going to school, playing music, drawing and creating things. I had a great childhood, and was blessed with many things. I never quite figured out what I wanted to be when I grew up, but I had to get a job to pay fof the things I needed and wanted. I found restaurant work to just come naturally, and moved up in the ranks quickly, always working hard and having great attention to detail and thoroughness.

I came to Austin Texas in 2010 to pursue a music career, but found out that there was a ton of competition. I fell back into restaurant work as it was easy to get a job at a restaurant. I helped open Haymaker in 2013, and became the Sous Chef in 2016. I have worked hard to get where I am, but want more for myself and am ready to make a career change that will benefit myself and my fiance.
